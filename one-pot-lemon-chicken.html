One-pot Lemon Chicken

Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min Total: 30 min
Easy Mediterranean
dinnerquickgluten-freelow-carb

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories320
Protein28g
Carbs4g
Fat22g
Fiber1g

Bright, savory chicken with lemon and herbs — all in one pan

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Sear chicken in a pan.
  3. Add lemon, herbs and roast until cooked through.
