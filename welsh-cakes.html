Welsh Cakes

Ingredients

  • 228 grams of Self-raising flour
  • 80 grams of Butter
  • 34 grams of Lard
  • 58 grams of Currants
  • 86 grams of castor Sugar
  • 1 egg
  • Mixed Spice

Steps

  1. Rub in butter with flour add sugar, fruit, mixed spice and egg
  2. Roll out dough on a floured board to about 2 a centimetre thick
  3. Use cookie cutter to cut out circles and cook them on medium heat for 4-5
  4. minutes each side (When touched during cooking they should feel springy, but
  5. not wet to the touch)
  6. Dust with castor sugar and enjoy
