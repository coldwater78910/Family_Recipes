Chicken Curry

Prep: 10 minutes Cook: 30-45 minutes Total: 40-60 minutes
easy-to-follow Indian

Simple chicken curry with rice and spring onion garnish

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 tin of coconut cream
  • 1 tin of diced tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of crushed garlic
  • Oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of chicken spice
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Squeeze of lemon juice or 1/2 a lemon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 cup rice
  • A spring onion (for garnish)

Steps

Curry

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into strips and place in a bowl.
  2. Pour a dash of oil over the chicken breasts.
  3. Add all the spices, garlic and lemon juice into the bowl with the chicken.(besides cloves and bay leaves)
  4. Cover with a plate and let marinade for 30+ minutes.
  5. Begin heating your pot while you dice your onion.
  6. After 5 minutes your pot should be thoroughly heated, add a dash of oil to the pot and let heat.
  7. Add your onion in and cook till it's translucent.
  8. Add in your sugar (be careful not to caramelize the onions or burn the sugar).
  9. Add in your marinated chicken breast, and sauté until outside is browned.
  10. Add in the tin of diced tomatoes, and cook down for 5 to 10 minutes.
  11. Add in your tin of coconut cream, bay leaf and cloves.
  12. Add the cubed potatoes and cook until tender.
  13. Once potatoes are tender and curry has thickened, taste and adjust seasoning as needed.

Rice

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Place rice in a pot with 6 cups of water bring to a boil
  3. When starch bubbles begin to seep out from under the lid, boil for 14 minutes.
  4. After the 14 minutes, drain of the excess water and set the rice aside

Serve the curry over rice and garnish with sliced spring onion.

Notes

  • For marinades, any amount of time for it to sit and absorb is better than no time
