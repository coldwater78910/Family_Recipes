Fluffy pancakes perfect for a weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Whisk in milk and eggs.
  3. Cook on a hot griddle.
