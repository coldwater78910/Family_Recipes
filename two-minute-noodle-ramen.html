Two minute noodle Ramen

Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 5 minutes Total: 20 minutes
Easy Various

Nutrition (per serving)

CaloriesN/A
ProteinN/A
CarbsN/A
FatN/A
FiberN/A

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 packets of two-minute noodles
  • 1/2 a cup of stock
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • 1/3 of a tomato
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • Lemon juice
  • 1/2 a teaspoon of garlic/garlic flakes
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1 chicken fillet

Steps

Chicken

  1. Cut your chicken fillet into thin strips and put into a bowl; add your preferred chicken seasoning, a dash of oil, and a drizzle of lemon juice. Cover and set aside.

Sauce

  1. In a small bowl put 2 of the spice packets from the two-minute noodles and the garlic; add a touch of water (just enough to dissolve the spice and heat the garlic).
  2. Then add your soy sauce, mayonnaise, and 2 teaspoons of lemon juice.
  3. Mix well until combined.

Toppings

  1. Boil your eggs to your preference, and chill in ice water when done.
  2. Dice your onion and tomato into small cubes.
  3. Finely chop your spring onion.
  4. Put all of the toppings into their own containers/bowls.

Noodles & Finishing touches

  1. Cook your noodles until almost done, then drain the water.
  2. Heat up a pan and cook your chicken (you don't need to add oil to the pan).
  3. Take the cooked chicken out of the pan and place in a container.
  4. In the same pan put in your noodles, sauce and half of the chopped onions and tomato.
  5. Once the sauce, tomatoes and onion are fully incorporated into the noodles take them out and place in your bowl.

Plating

  1. Add the half cup of hot stock.
  2. Cut each of the eggs in half and place on top of the noodles on one side of the bowl.
  3. Take the slices of chicken and place them in the middle.
  4. Now you can put the rest of the diced tomatoes and onion on the opposite side of the bowl to the eggs.
  5. Enjoy!
