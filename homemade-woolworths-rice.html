Homemade Woolworths rice

Prep: 5-15 minutes Cook: 30± minutes Total: 35-45 minutes
easy Various

Nutrition (per serving)

CaloriesN/A
ProteinN/A
CarbsN/A
FatN/A
FiberN/A

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 6 cups of water
  • 100g of peas
  • 100g of corn
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tablespoons of Maggi Aromé All-Purpose Seasoning

Steps

  1. Rinse rice in cold water until water is clear
  2. Place rice in a pot with 6 cups of water
  3. Heat rice until water begins to boil and starch bubbles begin to seep out from under the lid, boil for 12 minutes.
  4. While rice is cooking, boil peas and corn in salted water
  5. Pull out peas and corn when they are almost finished cooking, and put them one side
  6. At this point your rice should be cooked, drain off excess water, and put the
  7. rice to one side
  8. Heat up your pan while you finely dice your onion, almost mincing it
  9. Drizzle oil into the pan once heated place in your diced onion
  10. When onion is translucent and has reduced, add in your peas and corn
  11. Add in your rice, and mix well
  12. 11.Cook for 5 minutes, and drizzle over 3 tablespoons of Maggi
  13. 12. Stir well, and enjoy
